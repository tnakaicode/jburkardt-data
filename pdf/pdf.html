PDF Files

PDF is a data directory which contains examples of PDF or "Portable Document Format" files, a format developed by Adobe Systems. While PDF is normally used for text or text plus illustration, we are here primarily interested in its use as a storage format for 2D images.

PDF File Characteristics:

  • binary
  • Color
  • 2D (page images)
  • Compression is possible
  • multiple images (pages)

Sample Files:

  • ajou_logo.pdf, a logo for Ajou University, in Suwon, Korea;
  • baboon.pdf, a grayscale image of a baboon;
  • bell_206.pdf, an image of a Bell Helicopter model 206.
  • cells.pdf, a single page black/white graphic;
  • charlie.pdf, a photograph of Charlie the dog;
  • coins.pdf, an image of coins;
  • football_seal.pdf, a dummy logo.
  • fsu_logo.pdf, a logo for Florida State University.
  • fsu_sports_logo.pdf, a sports logo for Florida State University;
  • icam_logo.pdf, a logo for the Interdisciplinary Center for Applied Mathematics at Virginia Tech.
  • pitt_logo.pdf, a logo for the University of Pittsburgh.
  • sample.pdf, a sample PDF file to be converted to PostScript by pdf2ps.
  • sc_logo.pdf, a logo for the Department of Scientific Computing at Florida State University;
  • scs_logo.pdf, the logo for the School of Computational Science at Florida State University (since renamed the Department of Scientific Computing).
  • ug_sec4.pdf, a few pages of text from a manual;
  • vt_logo.pdf, a circular logo for Virginia Tech.
  • vt2_logo.pdf, a rectangular logo for Virginia Tech.
  • yonsei_logo.pdf, a logo for Yonsei University, Korea.
